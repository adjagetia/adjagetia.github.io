Robotic Control of a Surgical Laser Waveguide using ABB Robot

Abstract

Automated tissue ablation offers many advantages over free hand surgical procedures as it is faster, more accurate, much efficient and offers great dexterity, particularly for complex ablations. This paper discusses the design, modeling and control of the ABB IRB 120 robot that will perform the task using a surgical laser waveguide. The laser waveguide will be attached to ABB IRB 120 end effector and will follow a desired trajectory with a given velocity to accomplish the task.
